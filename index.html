What is a Solar Panel System?

A solar panel system produces electricity from sunlight using photovoltaic cells. The electricity generated is first in DC form and is converted into AC power by an inverter so it can be used in homes, offices, and schools. Solar systems help reduce electricity bills, provide clean renewable energy, and protect the environment by reducing pollution.
